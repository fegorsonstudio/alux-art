# Alux Art — User Manual

## What is Alux Art?

Alux Art is an AI-powered photo studio. You upload selfies, pick a style created by a professional photographer or stylist, pay once, and receive high-resolution editorial portraits in about an hour. No photographer. No studio. No prompts to write.

---

## For Buyers

### Getting started
1. Go to **aluxartandframes.shop**
2. Click **Browse styles** or **Browse looks** in the navigation
3. You do not need an account to browse — only to book

### Creating an account
1. Click **Sign in** in the top-right corner of any page
2. Enter your email address and a password
3. Check your email for a confirmation link and click it
4. You are now logged in and ready to book a shoot

### Browsing styles
- The **Marketplace** page shows all published styles
- Use the **currency toggle** (NGN / USD) to view prices in your preferred currency
- Click any style card to see its full preview, sample images, and price breakdown

---

### Booking a shoot — step by step

#### Step 1 — Choose your package
On the template detail page, select either:
- **5 images** — smaller package, lower price
- **10 images** — full shoot, best value

#### Step 2 — Upload your identity photos
- Upload at least 3 clear selfies of your face
- Requirements: front-facing, good lighting, no sunglasses, no heavy filters
- These photos are used **only to identify your face** — the outfit you are wearing in these photos is completely ignored and will not appear in your portraits

#### Step 3 — Upload pose references (optional)
- Upload up to 10 reference photos showing angles or poses you want
- Examples: a back shot, a side profile, a full-body pose, a seated pose
- The AI will attempt to match those angles in your generated portraits
- If you skip this step, the AI chooses varied poses on its own

#### Step 4 — Review and pay
- Check the price summary shown at the bottom of the page
- Apply a coupon code if you have one (enter it in the coupon field and click Apply)
- Click **Pay & Generate** — you will be redirected to Paystack's secure checkout
- Pay using card, bank transfer, USSD, or OPay

#### Step 5 — Wait for your portraits
- After payment is confirmed, your shoot enters the queue automatically — you do not need to do anything
- Generation takes approximately 45 minutes to 1.5 hours depending on queue length
- You can close the page and come back later — your shoot will still be processing

---

### Viewing and downloading your portraits

1. Log in and click **Studio** in the top navigation (or go to aluxartandframes.shop/studio)
2. Find your shoot — the status badge will show **Generating**, **Complete**, or **Failed**
3. Once complete, click any image to view it full size and download it
4. Click **Download all (ZIP)** to download all your portraits at once as a single file

---

### If a portrait fails to generate

- Failed images show a **Retry** button next to them
- Click Retry to regenerate just that image — there is no extra charge
- The other images in your shoot are not affected
- If most or all images fail, contact support with your Shoot ID (visible on the Studio page)

---

### Coupon codes

- Enter your coupon code in the coupon field on the booking page before paying
- Coupons apply a discount to the platform fee portion of the price
- A coupon can only be used a set number of times — if it shows as invalid, it may have been fully used
- Coupon codes are case-insensitive

---

### Getting help

1. Scroll to the footer of any page and click **Support**
2. Fill in the contact form: your name, email, subject, and message
3. If your issue is about a specific shoot, include your **Shoot ID** — you can find it on the Studio page next to your shoot
4. You can also email directly: support@aluxartandframes.shop
5. Response time is typically within 24 hours

## For Creators

Creators are photographers, stylists, and art directors who build and sell style templates on the marketplace. Every time a buyer books your template, you earn a payout directly to your bank account.

### Applying to become a creator

1. Go to **aluxartandframes.shop/become-creator**
2. Fill in your profile:
   - Display name (what customers will see)
   - Bio (your background, specialties, aesthetic)
   - Instagram URL and/or website (optional but recommended)
3. Upload a profile photo
4. Enter your bank account details for payouts — these are verified securely via Paystack
5. Submit your application
6. An admin will review your application and approve or decline within a few days
7. You will receive an email when your status changes

---

### Your creator dashboard

Once approved, go to **Creator Dashboard** (link in the top navigation after logging in).

From the dashboard you can:
- View all your templates and their booking counts
- Check your total earnings and payout history
- Update your payout bank account
- Create new templates
- Edit or unpublish existing templates

---

### Building a template

#### Step 1 — Open the template editor
Click **New Template** in your creator dashboard.

#### Step 2 — Fill in the basics

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Title | Clear, descriptive name — e.g. "Lagos Street Editorial" |
| Category | Portrait, Fashion, Lifestyle, Beauty, etc. |
| Description | What the style looks like, the mood, the clothing direction, who it suits |
| Shoot mode | **Fast** (single inspiration image drives everything) or **Advanced** (tagged references for precise control) |
| Aspect ratio | 4:5 for Instagram portrait, 16:9 for landscape |

#### Step 3 — Upload reference images

This is the most important part. Your reference images define what every customer's shoot will look like.

| Tag | What it controls |
|-----|-----------------|
| No tag (Inspiration) | Sets the overall mood, outfit, lighting, background, and creative direction |
| `[OUTFIT]` | Replaces the inspiration outfit with this specific look — used in all portraits |
| `[BACKGROUND]` | Uses this image as the environment or backdrop |
| `[LIGHTING]` | Matches this lighting setup |
| `[HAIRSTYLE]` | Applies this hair reference to the subject |
| `[MAKEUP]` | Applies this beauty/makeup look |
| `[ACCESSORY]` | Adds these accessories (jewellery, bags, hats) |
| `[COLOR_GRADE]` | Applies this film or editing color style |

**Minimum:** Upload 2–4 strong **inspiration** images (no tag). These define the core look and appear in your style preview on the marketplace.

**Advanced mode tip:** Use `[OUTFIT]` to lock a specific outfit across all portraits so it does not change from pose to pose. Use `[BACKGROUND]` when you want a specific environment that the inspiration image does not show clearly.

#### Step 4 — Set your prices

- **10-image price (required):** Your standard full-shoot price in NGN
- **5-image price (optional):** Price for the smaller package — if left blank, the 5-image option will not be available for your template
- The platform deducts its fee from each booking and transfers your share to your bank account automatically

#### Step 5 — Save and publish

- Click **Save as Draft** while you are still refining — the template is not visible to customers yet
- When you are satisfied, click **Publish** — the template appears on the marketplace immediately
- You can unpublish at any time from your dashboard

---

### Editing a template

1. Go to **Creator Dashboard** → find your template → click **Edit**
2. You can update the title, description, prices, and reference images at any time
3. All changes go live immediately
4. Changing reference images will affect future shoots booked from that point — existing shoots in progress are not affected

---

### Understanding your earnings

- Every booking creates a record showing: total paid by the customer, platform fee, and your payout amount
- Your payout is transferred to your bank account via Paystack after each successful payment is confirmed
- Payouts appear in your bank account within 1–3 business days depending on your bank
- You can see a full history of bookings and payouts in the **Earnings** section of your creator dashboard

## For Admins

Admins have full access to the **Admin Panel** at `/admin`.

### Creator management

- **Pending applications:** Review new creator applications — approve or decline
- Approved creators immediately gain the ability to publish templates
- Declined applicants can reapply after updating their profile

### Template oversight

- View all templates across all creators (including drafts)
- Suspend a template if it violates content guidelines — it is hidden from the marketplace but not deleted

### Pricing and configuration

- Set the platform fee in NGN via the **Pricing** section
- The fee scales proportionally by package size automatically
- Use `test_price_per_image_ngn` in app_config to override prices for testing — delete the row to restore real prices

### Coupon codes

- Create coupon codes: set the code, discount type (percent off platform fee or fixed NGN amount), expiry date, and max usage count
- Deactivate a coupon at any time without deleting it
- Monitor usage count per coupon in the admin panel

### Template Lab

The **Template Lab** at `/admin/template-lab` is an admin-only workspace for testing templates before publishing.

- Select any published template
- Run a test generation to verify the output looks correct
- Use this to quality-check new creator templates before approving them for the main marketplace

### Shoot debugging

Each shoot has a **Debug** view accessible from the admin panel.

- Shows a full timeline of generation events
- Displays current status, progress percentage, and pipeline stage
- Shows error messages if generation failed
- Use this to diagnose customer issues — always ask for the Shoot ID when a customer reports a problem

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Will the portraits look exactly like me?**
Yes — the AI is specifically configured to preserve your face shape, skin tone, eye spacing, nose shape, and overall likeness. What changes is the outfit, lighting, background, and pose.

**Can I get full-body shots?**
Yes — upload a pose reference photo showing the full-body angle you want, and the AI will attempt to match it. The more specific your pose references, the better the result.

**My portraits don't look like me — what do I do?**
Contact support with your Shoot ID. Include 1–2 example portraits from your shoot and describe what looks different. We will investigate and may offer a complimentary retry.

**Can I use the portraits commercially?**
Yes — all portraits you generate are yours to use freely for personal and commercial purposes.

**How long does a shoot stay in my account?**
Completed shoots and their images are retained in your account. You can download them at any time.

**I paid but nothing is happening.**
Check the Studio page and look for your shoot. If the status shows **Pending Payment**, the payment may not have confirmed yet — wait 10 minutes and refresh. If it still shows Pending Payment after 10 minutes, contact support with your Shoot ID and the payment reference number from your bank.

**Can I get a refund?**
Once a shoot has started generating, refunds are not available. If the shoot fails entirely (all images fail), contact support.

**How do I become a creator?**
Go to aluxartandframes.shop/become-creator, fill in your profile and bank details, and submit your application. Approval takes a few days.
